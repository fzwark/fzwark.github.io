Research

I am broadly interested in efficient algorithm design, particularly in developing novel algorithms for accelerating LLM (and agent) inference and reducing computational costs with formal theoretical guarantees. My research bridges theoretical insights and practical design by integrating effective and provably efficient algorithms into cutting-edge LLM-based agents and applications.
